Finance Research Forum (Finance ERF) aims at conducting practical and in-depth research by encouraging exchanges of information among financial experts. The forum consists of outside experts, including professors and researchers, as well as members of the Financial and Monetary Economics Team of the BOK Economic Research Institute.



The forum is held at least once a quarter to present academic papers or discuss on pressing policy issues. In addition, at the forum, experts at home and abroad are invited to share their insights into recent issues on finance research.



If you are interested in joining the forum, please contact Financial and Monetary Economics Team (82-2-759-5395, hibkkim@bok.or.kr).
