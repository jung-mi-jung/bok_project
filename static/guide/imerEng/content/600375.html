Introduction

BOK Economic Research Institute operates an economic advisory panel to strengthen its research capabilities and enhance its internal and external reputation through acquiring diverse academic information and reinforcing collaboration with prominent scholars.
The economic advisory panel provides consultation on various aspects of the institute's research activities. In particular, it can offer opinions on papers and reports published by the institute, and provide assistance in organizing and conducting seminars and academic conferences hosted by the Economic Research Institute.
