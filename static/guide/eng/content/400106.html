FIP Archievements

Over the years, the CFIP has successfully spurred innovation of the financial industry through its common IT projects harnessing the latest technologies. It has constructed a shared infrastructure for the financial sector, developed solutions and standards for its safe and efficient use, and made sustained efforts to increase the financial inclusion of vulnerable groups and underserved communities that can be left behind by the digital transformation of finance.

Non-Bank Financial Industry

Informatization of the non-bank financial industry has also been steadily promoted since the mid-1970s. The securities industry has constructed various information systems, such as the Transaction Contraction System, the Futures Trading System, the Stock Price Index Futures System, the Stock Price Index Options System, etc. In the late 1990s, an online stock trading system was developed, which in recent years has handled more than half of total stock trading activities.

The insurance industry has constructed various inquiry systems in the car insurance field, the High Value Contracts Risk Management System, the Traffic Law Contravention Information Exchange System, etc.
