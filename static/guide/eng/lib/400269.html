Bank of Korea Money Museum Busan Branch

The Bank of Korea Busan Branch invites you to visit our Money Museum, which offers visitors a wealth of opportunities to learn about the central bank and to explore the history of money. The museum displays over 600 domestic and foreign banknotes and coins, including many rare items, and also has many fun and interactive exhibits for people of all ages to enjoy.
