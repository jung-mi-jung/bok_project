Bank of Korea Money Museum Gyeongnam Branch

The currency exhibition room at the BOK's Gyeongnam branch displays about 450 examples of Korean and foreign currencies from the ancient times to the present. The room also details the life of currency, from birth to death. Visitors are also provided an opportunity to identify fake money.
