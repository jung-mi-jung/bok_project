Bank of Korea Money Museum Chungbuk Branch

The currency exhibition room at the Chungbuk branch of the Bank of Korea displays a total of about 1,000 exhibits including Korean currencies of various times, from ancient commodity currencies to currencies now in circulation, and commemorative coins and currencies from foreign countries. There is also a section dedicated to economics education, to promoting public understanding of the history of Korean and foreign currencies.
