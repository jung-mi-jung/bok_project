Bank of Korea Money Museum Daegu & Gyeongbuk Branch

The currency exhibition room at the Daegu & Gyeongbuk branch of the Bank of Korea consists of Korean currency zone, showcasing Korean currencies in the chronological order, and of World currency zone, displaying currencies from 65 countries by theme. This room presents a total of more than 1,900.
