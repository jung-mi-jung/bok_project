The Bank of Korea’s leadership consists of the following: the Monetary Policy Board, which deliberates and decides on matters related to monetary policy and the operations of the Bank; the executive body, comprising the Governor, the Senior Deputy Governor, the Deputy Governors, the head office divisions, domestic branches, etc.; and the Auditor, whose role is to regularly audit the Bank’s business operations.
