BANK OF KOREA MONEY MUSEUM

Visitor Guides

  • Opening hours : Tuesday to Sunday, 10:00 ~ 17:00
  • Closed : Mondays, Labor day(5.1), Dec. 29 ~ Jan. 2, Election day, Lunar New Year holiday and Chuseok(Korean Thanksgiving Day) holiday
  • Admission Fee : Free entry
  • Parking facilities : Unavailable (Public transportation is recommended)
  • To visit on weekend and holiday, reservation is needed in advance : through our website, 30 min per 70 people.
  • Group visits : Groups of twenty people or more, please make a reservation at least one day in advance.  Student groups should be accompanied by teachers.
  • Contact : 02)759-4881, 4882
  • Website :museum
  • Address : 39, NAMDAEMUNNO, JUNG-GU, SEOUL, KOREA

Guided tours

Explore the Korean money displayed at the museum with docent

  • Tour lasts for 30 minutes
  • Each tour has a capacity of 30 people.
  • Tours are free but booking is needed. You can book through our website in advance. Also you can book at the information desk, if there is a capacity left.
  • Individual Visitors : Available three times a day(11:00, 14:00 and 15:00)
